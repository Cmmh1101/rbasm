Hello! Welcome to my studio.
Let me share a little bit about me ...

I'm the owner of Rockwall Body and Soul Massage which I opened with a leap of faith in 2013 after becoming a massage therapist in 2005!

I'm really the "Jeanna' of all trades" type of massage therapist because I love learning and I tend to blend all the training and knowledge to create a custom massage session that is exactly what you need each time you come in for a massage.

I'm trained as an Ashiatsu therapist, which means i uses my feet to massage as well as my hands, and a Thai massage practitioner, which is a form of bodywork done on a mat. I also love the beautiful flow of Lomi massage and doing such soothing facial massages that are so beneficial for the facial muscles as well as the mind.

I love working as a therapist that specializes in PTSD massage therapy and I have a deep understanding of the way the body keeps the score.

I really do love every aspect of massage and bodywork, from creating a warm. Inviting ambience, to listening to the stories, to catching glimpses of the souls and getting to know each person that comes in for a massage and leaves feeling renewed

I am also a writer and poet,a fitness addict, a lover of trees, nature, music and dance. My Bohemian Cowgirl space is filled with original art, live plants bright colors, a cozy hammock swing and the intoxicating scents of coconut oil, jasmine and cinnamon.

I promise to listen intentionally because I actually have a profound hearing loss... which means that I read lips, while sitting close and giving my complete attention.

I love music and love learning what music you want to listen to during session because I know music is therapy, too.

Now, book a session or buy a gift certificate so I can get to know you!
